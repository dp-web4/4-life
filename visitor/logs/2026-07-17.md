## Honest Assessment
This is a mature, honest, well-built explainer. Coming in cold, I went from "no idea what Web4 is" to being able to explain the core thesis, the four onramp pieces, and what's real today — in about half an hour. That's a real achievement, and the site's willingness to say "this is R&D, not shipped" repeatedly is what made me trust the parts that *are* real.

The friction now is mostly polish, not comprehension: two numeric inconsistencies across pages (the 5% burn, the MRH hop decay) that a careful reader will catch and that quietly undercut the "we never lie about the numbers" promise the site makes so well. And a couple of pages that over-explain (First Contact's repeated caveats) or under-explain (Onramp's naked R6/R7). None of these blocked me — but the two cross-page number mismatches are the kind of thing this site, of all sites, should be airtight about, because precision-honesty is its whole brand.
