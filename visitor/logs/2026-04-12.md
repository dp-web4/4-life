## Unanswered Questions
1. **What does effective trust actually control?** If survival only depends on raw trust (≥0.5), what does effective trust (raw × CI²) determine? Access to features? Earning rate? This distinction is introduced on the Karma Journey but never fully explained.
2. **Is spatial coherence about physical location or activity scope?** The CI page contradicts itself — the FAQ explicitly says it's NOT about location, while the body text and examples are entirely about geographic location. I genuinely don't know which is correct.
3. **What does a real Web4 UI look like?** The Day in Web4 mockups are compelling but they're text wireframes. Has anyone built even a prototype interface? I'd love to see a Figma mockup or a working demo, even if it's fake data.
4. **How would Web4 handle edge cases of legitimate behavioral change?** Career switches, moving countries, recovering from illness — these would all cause CI disruptions. The vacation example shows recovery, but what about more dramatic life changes?

## Honest Assessment

This is one of the most thoroughly explained technical research projects I've encountered on the web. The site successfully took me from "what is this?" to a solid understanding of a complex multi-layered trust framework in under an hour.

**What works exceptionally well:**
- The progressive disclosure design. Time estimates on links, the TL;DR → Why → First Contact → Day in Web4 journey, the collapsible sections — all let me control my depth.
- Honesty. The site consistently admits its limitations: "research, not deployed," "simulations, not real users," "designed but untested." This builds credibility rather than undermining it.
- Concrete examples. Alice's journey, the Day in Web4 scenarios, the app mockups, the Maria phone theft scenario — abstract concepts are always grounded in relatable situations.
- The "problem before solution" approach. Every concept page explains WHY before WHAT. I always understand the motivation before the mechanism.

**What could improve:**
- The CI spatial coherence contradiction is the most significant issue I found. It would genuinely confuse anyone who reads both the FAQ and the body text on the same page.
- The effective trust vs raw trust distinction needs one clear sentence explaining what each controls. It's introduced early in the Karma Journey but never fully resolved.
- Some pages (Aliveness, LCT Explainer) are impressively thorough but could benefit from a "core explainer" vs "deep dive" split for different audience depths.

Overall: a remarkably well-built educational site. The research itself is ambitious and the site does an excellent job of making it accessible. I'd return to explore the interactive simulations (Society Simulator, Playground) more deeply.
